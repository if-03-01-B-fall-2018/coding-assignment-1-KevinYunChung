Topics related to VR

Here are some interesting topics which are Virtual Reality related.

Virtual Reality

Virtual reality (VR) is an interactive computer-generated experience taking place within a simulated environment. It incorporates mainly auditory and visual feedback, but may also allow other types of sensory feedback like haptic. This immersive environment can be similar to the real world or it can be fantastical.

Augmented Reality

Augmented reality (AR) is an interactive experience of a real-world environment where the objects that reside in the real-world are "augmented" by computer-generated perceptual information, sometimes across multiple sensory modalities, including visual, auditory, haptic, somatosensory, and olfactory.

Simulated Reality

Simulated reality is the hypothesis that reality could be simulated—for example by quantum computer simulation—to a degree indistinguishable from "true" reality. It could contain conscious minds which may or may not be fully aware that they are living inside a simulation.

History of Virtual Reality

The exact origins of virtual reality are disputed, partly because of how difficult it has been to formulate a definition for the concept of an alternative existence. Some say thst parts indicating to VR go back to the 1860s. Still the origins to VR is still twisted.

The Virtual Reality market is mainly being used by the Gaming Industry, but it could find its way to the Educational part of the world. It is also being used in Entertainment like movies, Health care for example a simulated surgery. Also the military uses it to simulate a battlefield for example

Future of VR Technology

The Future of Virtual Reality is still not clear. But there are some speculations on how it can be developed for example bringing cinema to life or being in a flight. VR is still being developed further but it could very well be the key for a Simulated Reality.
